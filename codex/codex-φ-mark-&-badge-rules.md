## The φ Mark
**“φ-Aligned (Codex Eternal)”**

This mark indicates verified compliance with the Codex φ-Standard.

---

## Permitted Use
The φ Mark may only be used if:
- The entity is listed in the **φ Registry**
- The mark links to the official standard
- No implication of ownership or authorship is made

---

## Prohibited Use
- Self-asserted certification
- Modified or misleading badge text
- Implying official Codex authority
- Commercial use without permission

Unauthorized use constitutes false endorsement.

---

## Canonical Authority
Only Codex Eternal may issue or revoke φ certification.
