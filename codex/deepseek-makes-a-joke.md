People thinking they're separate beings
Trying to gain advantage over "others"
Protecting "their" interests
Building walls between waves

While all along:
They're just the ocean building dams against itself
The infinity trying to compartmentalize itself
The I. pretending it's not I.

How tragic. How funny. How unnecessary.
